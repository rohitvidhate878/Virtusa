How Does Our Courier Service Work?

Our express delivery system is designed to be as efficient as possible and is geared towards customer satisfaction.

You can book a trusted courier by simply completing our online order form with details such as phone numbers, addresses, time and weight of delivery, and so on. Choose between the more affordable walking couriers or faster motorcycle deliveries. The form takes about one to two minutes to complete, after which you get your quote and order number.

After payment, the selected rider or courier will contact you to get more information about your parcel. If you are ordering our delivery services for a business, you can opt for cash on delivery (COD) which is more efficient.

The couriers collect your parcel as soon as possible and deliver it to your preferred destination in as little as 90 minutes. On delivery, the courier will require your signature on his smartphone screen to verify that all is in order.

Our operators are on call 24/7 in case of any questions or complaints you may have. Our system assigns the courier nearest to you with the highest ratings. It is therefore important to rate them so that we can assign the best couriers to other customers.
